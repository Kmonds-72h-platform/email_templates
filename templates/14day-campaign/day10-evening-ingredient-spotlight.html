üåü Star Ingredient Deep Dive

üî¨ Peptides - The Skin Communication System
What they are: Short chains of amino acids that act as messengers, telling skin cells how to function
Types: Signal peptides (boost collagen), carrier peptides (deliver minerals), neurotransmitter peptides (reduce wrinkles)
Key Benefits: Stimulate collagen production, improve skin firmness, reduce fine lines, enhance repair processes
Usage Tips: Use consistently for 8-12 weeks, apply to clean skin, can be layered with other actives
üß¨ Snail Secretion Filtrate - Nature's Repair Complex
What it is: Mucin produced by snails containing glycoproteins, hyaluronic acid, and antimicrobial peptides
Concentration: Effective at 90%+ concentration, lower percentages provide hydration benefits
Key Benefits: Accelerates healing, reduces inflammation, intense hydration, improves texture
Usage Tips: Safe for sensitive skin, use AM/PM, excellent for post-procedure healing
üåø Ginseng - The Energizing Adaptogen
What it is: Powerful adaptogenic root rich in ginsenosides that energize and protect skin
Types: Red ginseng (fermented, more potent), white ginseng (fresh), American ginseng (cooling)
Key Benefits: Boosts circulation, anti-aging properties, improves skin tone, protects against environmental stress
Usage Tips: Best in morning routines, enhances other actives, suitable for all skin types
üß™ Fermented Ingredients - Bioavailable Powerhouses
What they are: Ingredients processed through fermentation to break down molecular structure for better absorption
Common types: Fermented rice water, fermented green tea, fermented soy, fermented yeast
Key Benefits: Enhanced penetration, increased potency, better skin tolerance, prebiotics for skin microbiome
Usage Tips: Ideal for sensitive skin, can be used daily, excellent for barrier repair
